About Us

Novateur HR Hiring solutions

Novateur HR Solutions is one of the fastest growing executive search firms partnering with clients across sectors for catering to their talent requirements at all levels; the firm’s journey thus far reflects phenomenal growth in servicing clients

Managed by the experienced HR professionals having diversified experience in sectors like Pharma, BFSI, Retail, Telecom, E commerce and Poultry to name a few and also various HR functions like Talent Management, Training, Employee Engagement and Pay Roll Management.

Our Vision

Novateur was established in 2014 with a vision to find unconventional methods to tackle challenges related to human resource management. Our vision is to setup a world class professional organization, backed with strong compliance and ethics, in the field of HR solutions provider and consultancy.

Our Mission

To establish ourselves as the most preferred HR solutions provider in the country by helping our clients reduce their time, effort and cost on human resource management and to provide right opportunities to the right candidate in order to help them grow in their careers.
